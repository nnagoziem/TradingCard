Lion

Animal

Scientific name: Panthera leo

Lifespan: 10-15 years

Natural habitat: Savanna and grassland

Best food: Antelope

Brief Info: The lion is a large mammal of the cat family. Some large males weigh over 250kg. Today, wild lions live in sub-saharan Africa and Asia. Lions are carnivores and scavengers. They are also apex predators and are often called "the king of the jungle". Compared to other cats, lions are social. A group of lions is called a pride.
